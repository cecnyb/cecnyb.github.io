About Me

Hello! I'm Cecilia Nyberg, a final-year graduate student in Data Science and AI. I have a broad experience across various areas of AI, but I am especially interested in deep learning. My specific interest area is enhancing the practical usability of computationally expensive models. My recent work has focused on improving transferability through methods like meta-learning and reducing training time with efficient data acquisition techniques.

With a bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering and Management, I also have a strong interest in financial analysis and exploring how AI can optimize business decision-making and profitability.

I am currently doing my Master's Thesis at Ericsson, which involves optimizing energy usage in radios through time-series forecasting.

Feel free to check out my projects and experiences on this site!
