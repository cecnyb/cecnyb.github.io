About Me

Hello! I'm Cecilia Nyberg, a recently graduated Data Science and AI Engineer with broad experience across various areas of AI. I’m particularly interested in deep learning, with a focus on enhancing the practical usability of computationally expensive models such as large language models (LLMs). My recent work has involved improving model transferability using meta-learning, reducing training time through efficient data acquisition techniques, and lowering parameter precision while maintaining performance.

With a bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering and Management, I also have a strong interest in financial analysis and enjoy exploring how AI can support business decision-making and improve profitability.

I recently completed my Master’s thesis at Ericsson, where I optimized energy usage in radio units using time-series forecasting techniques. I’m currently a visiting researcher at the Deep Learning Lab, Department of Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering, Seoul National University. This position, which runs through February 2026, focuses on improving the energy efficiency of LLMs.

Feel free to explore my projects and experiences on this site!
