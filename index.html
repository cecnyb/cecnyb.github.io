About Me

Hello! I'm Cecilia Nyberg, a final-year graduate student in Data Science and AI. Although I have broad experience across various areas of AI, I am especially interested in deep learning and enhancing the practical usability of computationally expensive models. My recent work has focused on improving transferability through methods like meta-learning and reducing training time with efficient data acquisition techniques.

With a bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering and Management, I also have a strong interest in financial analysis and exploring how AI can optimize business decision-making and profitability.

I am currently doing my Master's Thesis for the company Ericsson, which involves optimizing energy usage in radios through time-series forecasting.
