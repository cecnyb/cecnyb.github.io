About Me

Hello! I'm Cecilia Nyberg, a last-year graduate student in Data Science and AI. My primary interest lies in deep learning and improving the practical usability of complex, computationally expensive models. I have recently explored methods for increasing transferability, such as meta learning, or lowering training time for example by using efficent data acquisition. I enjoy solving challenging problems and developing innovative solutions in AI.

With a bachelor's degree in industrial engineering and management, I also have an interest in financial analysis and exploring how AI and data science can optimize business profitability and decision-making.

I am currently doing my Master's Thesis for the company Ericsson, which involves optimizing energy usage in radios through time-series forecasting.
