About Me

Hello! I'm Cecilia Nyberg, a recently graduated Data Science and AI Engineer. I’m especially interested in deep learning and in making computationally expensive models—like LLMs—more practical.

My recent work includes improving model transferability with meta‑learning, lowering parameter precision while maintaining performance, and applying AI to radio units at Ericsson.

I completed my Master’s thesis at Ericsson in June, optimizing energy usage in radio units using time‑series forecasting. I’m currently a visiting researcher at the Deep Learning Lab at Seoul National University. Here I focus on software‑hardware co‑optimization (through February 2026).

Feel free to explore my projects and experiences on this site!
